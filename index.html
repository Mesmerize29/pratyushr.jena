What I do now

I work at the intersection of AI security, robotics and intelligent systems. I design safety aware pipelines and autonomous behaviors that stay stable in messy environments.

What I have done before

Drones, hexacopters, optical flow, LiDAR navigation, vial classification, dialogue risk models, university grade research projects and hands on systems debugging from the lab bench up.

What I want next

I want to scale secure AI and autonomy into products, labs and research groups that care about reliability. My work aims at systems that learn fast, defend themselves and support human decision making.
