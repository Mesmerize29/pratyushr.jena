What this portfolio is built around

The common thread in my work is not one narrow title. It is the kind of problems I keep getting pulled toward: systems that touch the real world, interfaces that shape human behavior, and technical stacks where reliability matters.

AI security and trust

I study how people and systems interact with AI under uncertainty, especially where trust, over-reliance, and poor interaction design can produce security failures.

Robotics, sensing, and autonomy

I have repeatedly worked on drone and perception systems where software, sensors, control, and physical constraints all need to line up for the system to be usable.

HCI and operational realism

I care about how technology actually behaves around people, teams, workflows, and real constraints rather than how it looks in a clean demo.
