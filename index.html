How I work

I am most useful on hard-edged work: ambiguous systems, unstable integrations, technical writeups that need precision, or products where the human layer is part of the failure surface. I care about clarity, reliability, and real constraints.

What anchors my work

The thread across my projects is not a single job title. It is the kind of problem I keep moving toward: systems where behavior, trust, sensing, and implementation all interact.

AI security and trust

I work on questions around unsafe reliance, trust signaling, and how human interaction with AI systems can quietly create security risk.

Robotics and perception

I have repeatedly worked on flight systems, sensing stacks, and camera-based workflows where hardware and software only matter if they hold up under actual conditions.

Human-centered systems

I care about interfaces, workflows, and operational environments because poor design in those layers often becomes the real point of failure.
